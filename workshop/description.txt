[h1]Apocalipse [BR] - PvP System[/h1]

A multiplayer-focused PvP shield system for Project Zomboid (Build 42).

[h2]How It Works[/h2]
- Craft and equip the Basic PvP Shield in the head slot.
- While shield HP is above zero, incoming player-vs-player hit damage is absorbed by the shield instead of your character.
- Each blocked hit reduces shield HP.
- If shield HP reaches zero, protection ends until HP is restored.

[h2]Out-of-Combat Regeneration[/h2]
- Shield regeneration starts only after you stay out of combat for the configured delay.
- Regeneration then restores shield HP every second based on the configured regen rate.
- Regen pauses again as soon as you take another PvP hit.

[h2]Server/Sandbox Control[/h2]
- Shield system can be enabled/disabled.
- Max shield HP can be customized.
- Regen rate and regen delay are configurable.

[h2]Balance Update[/h2]
To make your PvP shield:
- Electronics Scrap: 30
- Sheet Metal: 20
- Duct Tape: 10
